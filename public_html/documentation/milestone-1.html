Shaya's Personal Website Project | Milestone One

Purpose, Audience, Goal

Purpose:

The purpose of my personal website project is to create a website that will serve as my personal portfolio with a focus on front-end web development.

Audience:

My site will be viewed by industry professionals, recruiters, hiring managers, peers, and potential clients who want to learn more about my background and experience.

Goals:

The primary purpose of this site is to serve as a web-based portfolio and to include a "contact me" opportunity via my contact form. Additional goals include acquiring full-time employment, sharing previous work experience, and showcasing my skills & personality on my site.

Persona

Name: Tara Cordon

Age: 47

Profession: Engineering Manager of CyberLog.com, a cybersecurity website looking to hire a full-time Junior Full-Stack Engineer. They are a large remote-based work environment that employs 300+ people globally.

Technology: Uses a 2023 MacBook Pro and an iPhone 15.

Attitudes & Behaviors: Short on time, but interested in finding people with a combination of both technical skills and soft skills so she can create a well-rounded team. Tara spends 3-5 minutes looking at an applicant's portfolio site, and only about one minute on a resume. Tara typically seeks potential candidates through LinkedIn, which is the only social site she uses consistently.

Frustrations & Needs: Tara prefers to hire someone with 2-3 years of development experience who will start at a competitive entry-level salary. CyberLog is a leader in the cybersecurity industry and can offer full-benefits. Tara gets frustrated when she takes the time to view a candidate's site, but still doesn't get a feel for someone's personality and full skill-set.

Goals: Tara wants someone who will be a great fit for her team and will grow with the company.

User Story: As a user, I need to determine quickly whether this person is a candidate for the entry-level Junior Full-Stack Engineer position.
